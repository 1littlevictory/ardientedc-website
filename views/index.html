About Ardiente

The name is Spanish for passionate, but in the DMV, Ardiente has become synonymous with precision, musicality, and style.

The team was founded in 2016 by six young, dedicated dancers with over 10 years of experience in creating choreography, performing and teaching Latin dance.

Although all Ardiente dancers come from different backgrounds, they are brought together by their passion for dance. The goal of Ardiente Dance Project is to introduce a fresh new perspective to the dance community by expressing everyone's unique and energetic style.

Professional Choreography

Ardiente is run under the direction of David Cuevas, who has been dancing salsa since age 14, and who was named the 2017 Instructor of the Year at the fifth annual DC Latin Honors Night ceremony.

Known for creating intricate and elegant turn patterns, accompanied by sharp stylish footwork, it's no surprise that the team has been turning heads at congresses nationwide, including Houston, Chicago, Washington DC, Tampa, and Charleston.

Ardiente Student Team

The Ardiente Student Team offers intensive training for passionate dancers ready to take their technique and performance skills to the next level.

Members train and practice alongside the pro team, and have the opportunity perform at congresses, socials, and salsa clubs in the DC area.
