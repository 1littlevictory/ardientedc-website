Ardiente Dance Company 3rd Year Anniversary Party

Description

Ardiente Dance Company is proud to announce that we have been going strong for 3 years! Performing, teaching, traveling, learning, social dancing, networking, costume designing, fundraising...you name it, we've done it!

Come celebrate with us as we enter our 3rd year as a dance company in the DMV area.
